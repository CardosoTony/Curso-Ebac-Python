Open World Discover the world of Sanctuary as you continue the battle against the Burning Hells. Join other adventurers, fight demonic forces, and uncover lost secrets.

World Bosses

World Bosses are encountered in open-world areas and consist of multi-stage battles. Players have to work together to defeat them (up to 12 players can engage in the battle at a time) and will be rewarded with unique items should they prevail.

Dungeons

As you progress through the story, explore over 120 dungeons overrun with monsters. Strike the monsters down and earn valuable experience and loot as a reward.

PVP

Battles take a turn when you enter PvP zones. Mark yourself as “hostile” and these high-risk, high-reward areas of Sanctuary allow you to take a swing at other players for their valuable loot. But be warned. This also opens you up to being swung at.
